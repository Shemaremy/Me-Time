MEET REMY

BIOGRAPHY

Greetings, I'm Shema Remy, a Rwandan native with a passion for technology and innovation. Born in 2003, my journey into the world of software engineering took an unexpected turn from my childhood dream of becoming a doctor. It was during my young age, spent obsessed in the realm of video games, that I discovered a curiosity for game development. A simple YouTube search at the age of 10 about how to make video games somehow awakened my interest, planting the seeds of my future in technology.

My academic journey led me through a comprehensive high school curriculum, where I studied subjects like Biology, Mathematics, Physics, and many more. However, it was the allure/charm of computer science that captivated me the most. From Grade 10 to 12, I found myself drawn to the complexities of coding and problem-solving, viewing it as more than just a subject but as a gateway to endless possibilities. Concepts like conditional statements and loops were my favourites, as I used to apply them in real-world scenarios.

As I approached Grade 12, my enthusiasm for computer science continued to grow. Eager to expand my skill set beyond the limitations of what we learnt the classroom, I embarked on self-directed learning during holidays, teaching languages like JavaScript. Upon completing high school, I reflected on my journey and aspirations, Considering the next steps in my academic and professional career.

While my university journey awaits, I seized the opportunity to further my knowledge through ALX, where I undertook courses in software engineering. Here, I deepened my understanding of web development and developed my proficiency in languages such as JavaScript, Python, SQL, and others. Additionally, I've also been an Irembo service agent in 2020 which provided valuable practical experience related to hands-on work, further driving my commitment to personal growth and development.

In essence, my journey thus far has been characterized by a determined pursuit of knowledge and an enthusiasm for technology. As I continue to chart my path forward, I remain dedicated to leveraging my skills and experiences to make meaningful contributions to the ever-evolving landscape of software engineering.
